Instructions

  • Enter your name and click Start Quiz to begin.
  • Customize the timer according to your preference(default is 30 seconds).
  • Listen the question. When ready, click Buzz! to answer.
  • Type your answer or use the microphone button to speak it.
  • Click Submit to check your answer.
  • Your score will be shown at the end. Click Start Over to try again.
  • Question with the answer will be shown at the end. You can share your results with others.
  • More Details can be found at Teen Talent
